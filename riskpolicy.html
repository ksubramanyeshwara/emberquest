Emberquest pvt ltd Risk Policy

Introduction

At Emberquest, we are dedicated to provide quality educational technology solutions while prioritizing the safety and security of our operations and stakeholders. This policy outlines our approach to risk management.


Risk Governance

A Risk Management Team will oversee risk management processes. Promote a culture of risk awareness and transparency among all employees.


Risk Identification

We will identify and assess:

(1)Operational Risks (e.g., technology failures, data breaches)
(2)Market Risks (e.g., competition, regulatory changes)
(3)Reputation Risks (e.g., negative media coverage, ethical concerns)


Risk Assessment

Classify risks as low, moderate, or high. Analyse potential impacts on our business.


Risk Mitigation

Develop specific risk mitigation strategies, including cybersecurity measures and compliance. Transfer risks through insurance or third-party agreements where appropriate.


Monitoring and Review

Continuously monitor and adjust mitigation strategies. Periodic reviews of this policy to address emerging risks.


Reporting

All employees are encouraged to promptly report identified or potential risks.


Compliance

All employees must adhere to this policy and relevant laws and regulations.


Conclusion

Emberquest is committed to proactive risk management to ensure the safety and success of our business.
